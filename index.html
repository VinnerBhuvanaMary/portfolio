Women's Day Celebration in College

About Women's Day Celebration

Women's Day is celebrated every year on March 8th to honor and celebrate the achievements and contributions of women in various fields. In our college, we take this day as an opportunity to appreciate and recognize the incredible women who are part of our academic community.

Event Details

Date: March 8, 2023
Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Location: College Auditorium

Activities

  • Guest lectures by inspiring women leaders
  • Panel discussions on women's empowerment
  • Cultural performances
  • Interactive workshops
  • And much more!

Join Us in Celebrating Women's Day

We invite all students, faculty, and staff to join us in celebrating Women's Day and recognizing the importance of gender equality and women's rights. Let's come together to empower and inspire each other!
